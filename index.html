Accolades, Achievements and Involvements

1st

IMDA NIC Face-Off App Development 2016

In a team of three, we created SocialShield, a web browser plugin which employs a revolutionary algorithm to protect users from harmful social media content. Our project eventually emerged at the top and qualified us for the APICTA Awards.

Merit

APICTA 2016

We were given the opportunity to participate in the Asia Pacific ICT Alliance (APICTA) Awards in December 2016. During our 5D4N trip to Taipei, we learnt new things, experienced new culture and most importantly, enjoyed ourselves. It was one of my most memorable experience in school.

2nd

IMDA NIC Face-Off Data Analytics 2016

This competition is significant to me for two reasons. Firstly, as a team leader, I was given the opportunity to demonstrate my leadership skills. Secondly, it introduced me to the subject of Big Data and data analytics, which gradually evolved into a sustained passion.

Merit

National Olympiad in Informatics 2017

For someone whose prior experience in programming is limited to Python, C++ was indeed a steep learning curve for me. But it was the prerequisite for NOI, so with little choice I grit my teeth and set about conquering it. The competition opened my eyes to a world beyond Python, where not everything is as smooth, simple and straightforward as desired. In the end, I was glad to acquire C++ skills, albeit at a rather basic level.

3rd

Code::XtremeApps:: 2016

It was my first experience in an infocomm-related competition. We were given 24 hours to develop an application revolving around Financial Technology. 24 hours. Sleep not included. It was hardcore. Nevertheless, we thoroughly enjoyed ourselves. After all, pulling an all nighter while representing the school is not something everyone gets to brag about.
